ABOUT

Welcome to the job board of the Corporate City State of Helsinki (CCSH)! We work with various partners to provide you with tasks that can be done from any location within the Helsinki metropolitan area provided with safe local network access point. Available jobs are displayed here together with information about the tasks, employers and immediate credit reward given for completing the task.

Outstanding performance in tasks may be rewarded with a possibility to apply for citizenship. Possible applicants will be informed immediately after a sufficient quantity of tasks has been completed. Unfortunately, we cannot inform you about the exact amount of tasks that must be performed to yield citizenship due to the differing nature and challenge level of the tasks. In addition, our partners at Dvergar Co. have agreed to lend surplus robotics equipment at a substantial discount for all registered users of the job board. This equipment may be retrieved from Dvergar's automated factories starting from the 19th of October, 2042.

By applying for a task you agree to a full non-disclosure agreement to keep Employer Confidential Information strictly confidential. You may not disclose the Employer Confidential Information to any person without the Employer’s prior written consent, and then only under conditions of confidentiality. Any breach of this non-disclosure agreement will lead to imminent suspension of your SIN (Secure Identity Number) account for anywhere from 1 month to 12 months.

Credits are paid directly to your cryptoaccount through our third-party verified servers by receiving cryptokey for your credit deposit. Full list of cross verification is available through our contact services. Your cryptoaccount credentials are received automatically by logging in to our site using automatic SIN account verification. SIN account verification has been provided to the CCSH by Huawei-Nokia router services. If you have no SIN account, you can create one automatically by submitting your DNA sample and retinal scan at the nearest SIN scanner station. Please take note that only inter-City transfers are allowed from this job board. Forwarding payments will lead to severe consequences due to City penal code no: 10923 and Finnish law (using the pre-revision version of 2036 where applicable). All rights are reserved.
